Our Heritage

Established in 2012

Founded by Filipino businessman Luis Rivera, Azure Mindoro was born from a vision to preserve the local highland aesthetic while introducing world-class luxury to the mountains. Built over two years, each beam was hand-picked from sustainable forest mahogany groves.

100% Sustainable
450+ Artisans Involved
Fun Fact

The resort's main lobby roof is over 12 meters high and was hand-woven using traditional Mindoro mountain techniques—taking over 6 months to complete!
